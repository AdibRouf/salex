Overview

This research presents a comprehensive model for autonomous vehicle decision-making in complex traffic scenarios. Our approach integrates advanced machine learning algorithms with real-time sensor data to enable intelligent navigation decisions. The model considers multiple factors including pedestrian behavior, traffic flow patterns, weather conditions, and road infrastructure to make optimal driving decisions. Through extensive simulation and real-world testing, we demonstrate the effectiveness of our approach in improving both safety and efficiency in autonomous vehicle operations. The system utilizes deep reinforcement learning combined with computer vision techniques to process environmental data and generate appropriate vehicle responses in real-time scenarios.
